Normalization in ADBMS

  • 1NF (First Normal Form): Eliminate repeating groups; ensure atomic values.
  • 2NF (Second Normal Form): Remove partial dependency; must be in 1NF.
  • 3NF (Third Normal Form): Remove transitive dependency; must be in 2NF.
  • BCNF (Boyce-Codd Normal Form): Stronger version of 3NF; each determinant is a candidate key.
  • 4NF (Fourth Normal Form): Remove multi-valued dependencies.
  • 5NF (Fifth Normal Form): Remove join dependency.
